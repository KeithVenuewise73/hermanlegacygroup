Our flagship platform and the companies around it

Herman Legacy Group is a professionally managed portfolio of companies, led by our flagship technology platform, Venuewise.

Venuewise Platform Featured Company · Flagship Platform

The Technology Backbone of Herman Legacy Group

Venuewise is the all-in-one sports technology platform at the center of the group — a single integrated system that powers the entire Huddle ecosystem and 5-Star Sports Media, connecting families, athletes, coaches, organizations, facilities, and tournaments.

The Huddle ecosystem — six connected products
HomeHuddleThe family sports calendar — every team, practice, and game synced in one place for the whole household.
AthleteHuddleAthlete profiles, stats, highlights, and recruiting tools that help players tell their story.
CoachesHuddleScheduling, rosters, and team communication built for the way coaches actually work.
OrganizationHuddleLeague and club management for organizations running multiple teams and seasons.
FacilityHuddleFacility scheduling, bookings, and open-slot management that keeps venues full.
TournamentHuddleTournament hosting, brackets, and event coordination from sign-up to final whistle.
